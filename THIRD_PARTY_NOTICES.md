## lsfg-vk and Lossless Scaling

This plugin does not bundle either project. It configures the lsfg-vk Vulkan
layer supplied by Batocera and detects a user-provided `Lossless.dll` copied
from a purchased Lossless Scaling installation.

- lsfg-vk: <https://github.com/PancakeTAS/lsfg-vk>
- Lossless Scaling: proprietary software from its respective copyright holder
